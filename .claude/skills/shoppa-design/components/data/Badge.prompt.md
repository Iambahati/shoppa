Compact label for statuses, categories and roles. Soft tinted fill + matching inset ring.

```jsx
<Badge color="emerald">Active</Badge>
<Badge color="amber">Awaiting verification</Badge>
<Badge color="purple">Verifier</Badge>     {/* staff roles use purple */}
<Badge color="stone" size="xs">Draft</Badge>
```

Tints: `stone | emerald | amber | red | blue | purple`. Sizes: `xs | sm | md`.
